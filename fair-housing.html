Our Commitment

Fair Housing

Equal Housing Opportunity

Lucas Winkler and Keller Williams Realty Metro are firmly committed to the letter and spirit of U.S. policy for the achievement of equal housing opportunity throughout the nation. We support an affirmative advertising and marketing program in which there are no barriers to obtaining housing because of race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, or national origin.

Equal Opportunity in Housing

Under the federal Fair Housing Act and the Michigan Elliott-Larsen Civil Rights Act, it is illegal to discriminate in the sale, rental, financing, or advertising of housing on the basis of the following protected classes:

  • Race or color
  • National origin
  • Religion
  • Sex (including gender identity and sexual orientation)
  • Familial status (families with children under 18, and pregnant persons)
  • Disability
  • Age, height, weight, and marital status (as protected under Michigan law)

How We Serve Every Client

We treat all clients, customers, and prospects with equal professionalism and respect. We do not steer buyers toward or away from neighborhoods based on any protected characteristic, and we provide the same quality of service and information to everyone we work with.

If You Have a Concern

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination, you have the right to file a complaint. You can contact:

  • U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD): hud.gov/fairhousing or 1-800-669-9777
  • Michigan Department of Civil Rights: michigan.gov/mdcr

Accessibility

We are committed to making our services accessible to all. If you need assistance or an accommodation to access information about properties or our services, please contact us and we will do our best to help.
